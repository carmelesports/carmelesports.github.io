ABOUT

What is Carmel Esports?

We are Carmel High School Esports. Created in fall 2018, we've grown from 1 Counter Strike player, 1 Hearthstone player, and a League of Legends playing teacher, to a tight-knit community of 100+ League of Legends, Counter Strike, Super Smash Brothers, Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch, Apex Legends, Rocket League, Hearthstone, Forza, and Fortnite players. With players of a semi-pro caliber, our club has established ourselves in the High School Esports League, Indiana High School eSports Network, Indy Gaming League and most recently the North American Scholastic Esports Federation.

What is Esports?

The Esports program we offer consists of games that have major pro scenes and require teamwork. Esports is competitive gaming at a high level of play. Basically like professional football or soccer, but for gaming instead. Some examples of what we offer are Rocket League, Overwatch League, and League of Legends. The benefits your child will get out of this club include leadership, social skills, team skills, potential scholarships, and other oppurtunities.
