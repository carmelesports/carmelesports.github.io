Carmel Esports 2021 Spring Tryouts

1/20/2021

With a successful start to the 2020-2021 school year where the Esports team acquired a dedicated room inside of the building as well as large tournament wins, Carmel Esports is ready to begin the reevaluation of rosters to continue our strong showing throughout all titles.

For all tryouts, please join the Tryouts Discord erver to communicate with other students and coaches. This Discord will be used to receive information regarding your game's tryouts as well as results. https://discord.gg/Zsf8UEumTD%3E

  • Super Smash Bros. Ultimate: 2/10 & 2/11 Online from 6:30-9 PM. Online bracket to determine top 4 spots of our 1v1 roster, then individual challenge matches to determine the last 6 spots of our 1v1 roster. 2v2 tryouts will be an online bracket only. Top 5 teams make the 2v2 roster.
  • Valorant: 2/12 Online from 6-9 PM. In-house scrimmages with coach evaluation. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Rocket League: 2/14 Online from 2-5 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 3 A Team, 3 B Team, & 3 C Team spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Rainbow 6: Siege Xbox 1: 2/14 Online from 2-5 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Rainbow 6: Siege PC: 2/13 & 2/14 Online from 5-7 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • League of Legends: 2/13 & 2/14 Online from 6-9 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 5 A Team, 5 B Team, & 5 C Team spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Overwatch (PC Only): 2/15 & 2/16 Online from 8-10 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 6 A Team, 6 B Team, & 6 C Team spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Smite: 2/20 Online from 2-5 PM. In-house scrimmage with coach evaluation. 5 Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.

The following titles are open for registration with unlimited roster spots. Please join the Discord for information on a callout meeting for these titles.

  • Apex: Legends (Console & PC | Duos & Trios)
  • Brawlhalla (1v1 & 2v2)
  • Online Chess (1v1)

Carmel Esports has a new home in G108 as a lab for the team. This will allow rosters to practice later into the season as computers and other hardware is set up!
