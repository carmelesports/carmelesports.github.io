CARMEL ESPORTS

What is Carmel Esports?

We are Carmel High School Esports. Created in fall 2018, we've grown from 1 Counter Strike player, 1 Hearthstone player, and a League of Legends playing teacher, to a tight-knit community of 80+ League of Legends, Counter Strike, Super Smash Brothers Ultimate, Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch, Apex Legends, Rocket League, Hearthstone, Forza, and Fortnite players. With players of a semi-pro caliber, our club has established ourselves in the High School Esports League, Indiana High School eSports Network, Indy Gaming League and most recently the North American Scholastic Esports Federation.
