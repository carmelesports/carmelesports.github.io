CARMEL ESPORTS

What is Carmel eSports

We are Carmel High School Esports. Created in fall 2018, we've grown from 1 CS:GO player, 1 Hearthstone player, and a League of Legends playing teacher, to a tight-knit community of 120 LoL, CS:GO, SSBU, R6, OW, Apex, RL, Forza, and Fortnite players. With players of a semi-pro caliber, our club has established ourselves in the High School Esports League, Indiana High School eSports Network, Indy Gaming League and most recently the North American Scholastic Esports Federation.
