Game Info

Rocket League is a vehicular soccer video game developed and published by Psyonix. This game utilizes cars and a team gameplay of mainly 3 versus 3 to create a competitive Esports scene of with over $1 million in prize pools. The game is fairly easy to pick up but very hard to master with an appeal for all ages as it isn't violent or gory. Rocket League focuses on teamwork, positivity, and practice to grow as a player and as a team.
