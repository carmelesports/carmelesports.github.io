Carmel Esports 2022 Spring Tryouts

Entering the Spring semester of the 2021-2022 school year, Carmel Esports is excited to pursue more state and national titles. This semester, multiple rosters will get the opportunity to travel to in-person events to compete for scholarship money and receive collegiate scouting. With these large events coming up, Carmel Esports will begin tryouts for the second semester in early January.

For all tryouts, please join the tryout Discord server to communicate with other students and coaches. This Discord will be used to receive information regarding your game's tryouts as well as results. https://discord.gg/JfyKT8523B

New Members

  • Visit E137 with any questions for Mr. Gentilcore regarding tryouts for the Spring semester
  • Make sure you join the Discord server linked above as important announcements are made there

Tryout Registration Process

  • To be eligible for tryouts, players must fill out the following Google Form which is open until January 10th at 4:30 PM:
    https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScKWdn0ML--E3PTrn048enhvgk3-j8vRg2Tya5CnrZYFtUoPQ/viewform?usp=sf_link
  • Players must also join the tryout Discord and request the proper roles for communication purposes
