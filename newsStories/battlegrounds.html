Carmel's New Tournament Series: Greyhound Battlegrounds Spring 2020 This Memorial Day Weekend

May 4th, 2020

Today Carmel Esports is proud to announce the details for the first tournament in what is planned to be long-running tournament series: Greyhound Battlegrounds. Greyhound Battlegrounds is an online tournament for high schools across the nation focused on providing the best esports competition possible. As one of the largest and most successful high school esports programs in the United States, Carmel Esports is looking to translate its experience winning National and State titles to organizing a new premier tournament series.

The free Greyhound Battlegrounds Spring 2020 is this Memorial Day weekend (May 23-25).

The Indiana High School Esports Network (IHSEN)-partnered event will feature Rocket League, League of Legends, & Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. A special Battlegrounds-exclusive exhibition featuring Valorant will take place afterwards to showcase a new up-and-coming esport!

Check out the trailer video!

Prize Pool

  • Valorant: 1st Place Plaque | $20+ per Player
  • Rocket League: 1st Place Plaque | $20+ per Player
  • League of Legends: 1st Place Plaque | $20+ per Player
  • SSBU: 1st Place Plaque | $20+ per Player
