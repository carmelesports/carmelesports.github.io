Carmel Places 2nd Once Again at Cardinal Open

May 4th, 2020

On April 25, 2020, Ball State University held its 2nd annual Cardinal Open, an esports tournament for high schools across the nation. After last year's 2nd place finish at the tourrnament in Fishers, Carmel once again made a title run in League of Legends during this year's online event.

Both Carmel Blue and Gold made deep runs to the winner's semifinals, but only Carmel Gold took their undefeated run to the championship match against Rector's List. Carmel Gold lost back-to-back Best of 1's vs Rector's List to claim 2nd once again at the tournament.

Carmel Esports would like to extend a huge thank you to Ball State, Cardinal Esports, and Dr. Walker for making this tournament happen!

By: Dylan Gentilcore
