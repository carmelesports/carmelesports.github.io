Carmel Esports 2020 Fall Tryouts

8/24/2020

After a year of national and state championships, massive club expansion, and growing community support, Carmel Esports is ready to kick off a full month of tryouts for all of our Varsity & Junior Varsity Fall teams.

For all tryouts, please check the information below & join our Tryouts Discord server: https://discord.gg/9ANKJep

  • Super Smash Bros. Ultimate: 8/31 (Carmel) & 9/3 (Greyhound) in Main Cafeteria 4-7 PM. Double-Elimination 1v1 bracket. Top 8 from each day make the team. 9/4 (Virtual Students Only) 4-7 PM. Double-Elimination 1v1 bracket. Top 4 make the team.
  • Hearthstone: 9/2 online 7-9 PM. 3 Varsity & 3 Junior Varsity spots available.
  • Rocket League: 9/3 & 9/4 online 7-9 PM. 3 A Team, 3 B Team, & 3 C Team spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • League of Legends: 9/9 & 9/10 online 7-9 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Valorant: 9/12 online 8-10 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Rainbow 6: Siege PC: 9/16 & 9/17 online 7-9 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Rainbow 6: Siege Xbox 1: 9/16 & 9/17 online 7-9 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Overwatch: 9/19 online 8-10 PM. 6 Varsity & 6 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Counter-Strike: Global Offensive: 9/21 online 7-9 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.
  • Smite (PC): 9/22 online 7-9 PM. 5 Varsity & 5 Junior Varsity spots available with sub spots at our discretion.

Callouts for the following titles are on 9/15 at 7 PM. You may sign up for these games without competing for a roster spot!

  • Apex: Legends (Console & PC)
  • Brawlhalla
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Gunfight 2v2 (PS4 & Xbox 1)
  • Fortnite
  • Forza Motorsport 7 (PC & Xbox 1)
  • Madden 21 (PS4 & Xbox 1)
  • NBA 2k21 (PS4 & Xbox 1)

Carmel Esports will soon have a new home in G108 with the launch of our new lab! Once open, tryouts, practices, and matches will be held there.

Tune in all week to https://www.twitch.tv/carmel_esports or visit our Watch page to check out some of our featured club streamers and learn more about tryouts!
