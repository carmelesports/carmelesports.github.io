Greyhound Battlegrounds Spring 2021

3/25/2021

Carmel Esports is back with Greyhound Battlegrounds for Spring of 2021! The Greyhound Battlegrounds is an online tournament for high schools across the nation focused on providing the best esports competition possible. For this upcoming Spring event, Valorant, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, Rocket League, and League of Legends will have tournaments with a combined prize pool of over $250!

Featuring the Following Games and Prizes

  • Valorant: 1st Place Plaque & $100 to the winning team
  • Super Smash Bros. Ultimate 1v1: 1st Place Plaque | $20 to the winning player
  • Rocket League: 1st Place Plaque & $60 to the winning team
  • League of Legends: 1st Place Plaque & $100 to the winning team

Mark Your Calendars!

Friday, April 23rd:

  • SSBU Start Time: 7 PM Eastern Time
  • Valorant Start Time: 7 PM Eastern Time

Saturday, April 24th:

  • Rocket League Start Time: 3 PM Eastern Time
  • League of Legends Start Time: 5 PM Eastern Time

Sunday, April 25th (Tentative Start Times):

  • SSBU Top 4 Playoff Bracket - 10 AM Eastern Time
  • League of Legends Top 4 Playoff Bracket - 12 PM Eastern Time
  • Rocket League Top 4 Playoff Bracket - 4 PM Eastern Time
  • Valorant Top 4 Playoff Bracket - 6 PM Eastern Time
