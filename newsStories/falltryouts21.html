Carmel Esports 2021 Fall Tryouts

With the start of the 2021-2022 school year, Carmel Esports will finally be able to take advantage of the Esports lab in G108 to compete against the best teams in the state, as well as in the nation. Additionally, that means Carmel Esports is ready to begin tryouts for the first semester of the 2021-2022 school year across various titles!

For all tryouts, please join the tryout Discord server to communicate with other students and coaches. This Discord will be used to receive information regarding your game's tryouts as well as results. https://discord.gg/JfyKT8523B

Callout Meeting

  • August 27, 2021 at 4 PM after school
  • If you are looking to try out for a roster, please attend this to acquire additional information, ask questions, and meet representatives from your game!

Tryout Registration Process

  • To be eligible for tryouts, players must fill out the following Google Form which is open until August 27th at 8 PM: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe--qM5E7z7aFgk8uT-1ptbsUwUammzeg_Wm27miT7cW14IRg/viewform?usp=sf_link
  • Players must also join the tryout Discord and request the proper roles for communication purposes

Supported Titles and Tryout Dates

League of Legends | 8/30 & 8/31 from 4:30-6:30 PM in G108

  • Up to 3 teams of 5-7 players determined through in-house scrimmages with coach evaluation
  • Players will be assigned to a tryout date(s) which they must show up to in order to make a roster

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive | 9/1 & 9/2 from 4:30-6:30 PM in G108
